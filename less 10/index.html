Twitter

Twitter is an American microblogging and social networking service on which users post and interact with messages known as "tweets".

Facebook

Facebook can be accessed from devices with Internet connectivity, such as personal computers, tablets and smartphones.

Linkedin

LinkedIn allows members (both workers and employers) to create profiles and "connect" to each other in an online social network.

Instagram

Instagram was originally distinguished by only allowing content to be framed in a square (1:1) aspect ratio with 640 pixels to match the display

You-Tube

YouTube is an American online video-sharing platform headquartered in San Bruno, California.

Tumblr

Tumblr (stylized as tumblr and pronounced "tumbler") is an American microblogging and social networking website founded by David Karp in 2007

Dribbble

Dribbble is a self-promotion and social networking platform for digital designers and creatives.
